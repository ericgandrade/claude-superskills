# startup-growth-strategist

Unified business authority for market validation, financial modeling, competitive strategy, and Go-To-Market execution.

## When to use

- Validating a startup idea or business model.
- Calculating market size (TAM/SAM/SOM).
- Projecting revenue and unit economics (LTV/CAC).
- Designing GTM strategies and Bet Boards.
- Analyzing competitive differentiation.

## What is included

- `SKILL.md` - Integrated growth strategy workflow.
- `templates/` - Market sizing methodologies and industry templates.
- `examples/` - Worked examples of SaaS and marketplace models.

## Typical invocation

- "Use startup-growth-strategist to calculate the market size for our new app."
- "Model our financial projections for the next 3 years."
- "Design a GTM Bet Board for our product launch."
