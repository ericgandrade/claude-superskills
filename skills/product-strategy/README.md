# product-strategy

Build product strategy that drives real choices. Use when defining where to play and how to win, creating strategy canvases, defining ICP/Anti-ICP, positioning products, setting pricing strategy, planning GTM motions, or structuring strategic bets. Part of the Modern Product Operating Model collection.

## When to use

- Strategic planning and execution.
- Product management workflows.
- Aligning teams on product architecture and delivery.

## What is included

- `SKILL.md` - Core workflow and instructions.
- `templates/` - Directory with supporting templates.

## Typical invocation

- "Use the product-strategy skill to help me plan this."
- "Apply the product-strategy framework to my project."
